# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 30, 2026**

## Overview

P360 ("we", "our", or "the app") is a workout decision tool that uses your Oura Ring data to provide training recommendations.

## Data We Access

When you connect your Oura Ring, we access:
- Daily Readiness Score
- Sleep Score
- HRV Balance
- Resting Heart Rate

## Data Storage

- **CLI Tool**: Your Oura tokens are stored locally on your device (`~/.p360/config.json`). We do not have access to this data.
- **Telegram Bot**: Your Oura token is stored in memory during your session. We do not persist or share this data.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect personal information
- We do not track your usage
- We do not sell or share any data
- We do not use analytics or tracking tools

## Third-Party Services

- **Oura API**: We connect to Oura's API to fetch your biometric data. Please review [Oura's Privacy Policy](https://ouraring.com/privacy-policy).

## Your Rights

You can disconnect your Oura Ring at any time:
- CLI: Delete `~/.p360/config.json`
- Telegram: Send `/disconnect`

## Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue on our GitHub repository.

## Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted to this page.
